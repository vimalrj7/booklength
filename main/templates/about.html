Book Length is a small project made by me, Vimal. Being an avid reader, I always found myself wondering how long it'd take me to finish the books I started, so I could make sure I complete my yearly goal. It came to mind mind that millions of people all over the world would probably be dealing with this problem too - hence, this website.

Book Length uses the Google Play Books API Family to show search results, and information about each book.

The amount of time required to finish is the book is calculated from the number of pages, the average number of words per page and the average human reading speed. All calculated values are hence a close estimate but are not 100% accurate.
